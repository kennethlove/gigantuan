Person

I'm Kenneth Love, a web developer and occassional web designer. I like to teach and I'm constantly perfecting.

I'm a huge fan of documentation, polyglotism, and strong opinions. I'm also a big believer in open source, both at Github and Bitbucket.

I'm also a husband, father, and hardline skeptic.

I have an Associates of Applied Sciences degree from Oklahoma State University, Okmulgee for Multimedia Design, and about 15 years of experience on the web, both amateur and professional.

I've worked a few different places, including Brick (as a contract web developer), Mercantila (as a software engineer), and SK+G Advertising (as an interactive developer). I've also been successfully freelancing continuously since 2009. I did it off of and on before then, too.

I do most of my work nowadays with my friend Chris Jones at our semi-sort-of startup Brack3t or on my own as my own company, Gigantuan (Who would've guessed?).
